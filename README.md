# DataGhost 

## Overview
DataGhost is a privacy-first "digital will" application designed to securely manage, preserve, and transfer a user's digital assets and accounts after they pass away. Built with stringent security measures, it ensures that sensitive digital legacies are only accessed by designated beneficiaries under the right conditions.

## Key Features
* ** Secure Asset Vault:** Encrypted storage for digital assets, passwords, and sensitive account information.
* ** Beneficiary Management:** Assign trusted individuals who will be granted access to specific digital assets upon verification.
* ** Privacy-First Architecture:** Designed with zero-knowledge principles to ensure data remains private until the designated trigger conditions are met.
